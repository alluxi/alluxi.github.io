About

This is a blog where I’m free to express my political views regarding social liberalism and why such a government is fair. I’ll be writing the posts anonymously, not because my opinions are extreme, but because this is a experiment.

You as a reader are free to use the comment area underneath each post, but keep in mind that every comment is processed manually. This will introduce some latency, but I believe that the discussion and the exchange of ideas will benefit from this as each commenter is “forced” to take a break in between verbal whiles.

I suggest that you with the first post and read your way through the blog. There are navigational links at the bottom of each blog post.
